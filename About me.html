Profile

My life has been filled with a diverse array of experiences. I love travel and have been lucky enough to combine work with this passion. This lifestyle allowed me to experience a multitude of cultures, to meet people from all walks of life, all over the world and an incredible path of unimaginable learning. My foremost life goal is to participate in variety and I am always in search of new experiences, new challenges and knowledge. I endeavor to help my fellow human out with their journey in life and because of this I tend to favour roles that are helpful to others. I enjoy diversity, being put out of my comfort zone and a challenge. I bring to every employer dedication, ambition, determination and believe myself to be a quick learner.
